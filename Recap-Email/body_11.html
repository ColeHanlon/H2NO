Helpful Tips

Observe Utah water levels

Within the H2NO mobile app, you can view current reservoir levels across the state of Utah.

Automatically detect recyclable items

Easily upload or take a photo within the H2NO mobile app of an item you are unsure about recycling.

Control your electricity use

Connect your Philips Hue smart light bulbs within the H2NO mobile app, and easily control them all in one place.
